Preference Elicitation Study: Traffic

Supporting Multi-Objective Decision Making.

Welcome to this experiment on multi-objective decision making for selecting parameters for traffic regulation in Amsterdam.

We built this web-interface to make the process easy, and appreciate the time you take to test it. The results used in this experiment are from the simulator, and averaged over 11 runs per parameter setting (of which there are 256). We removed all results that are dominated by other results, which left us with 75. The aim of this experiment is to find the best parameter settings, from those 75 results, according to your preferences.

Note: Javascript has to be enabled, and the experiments do not work on mobile.
